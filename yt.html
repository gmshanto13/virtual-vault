5 best ways to Make $10,000/Month With Youtube shorts

YouTube Shorts is a new feature that allows users to create short, 15-second videos. This feature has grown in popularity, and many creators are now making money with it. In this blog post, I will share my personal experience of earning $10,000 per month from YouTube Shorts.

1. Increasing your audience:
The key to making money with YouTube Shorts is to increase your audience. This can be accomplished by creating and sharing high-quality, engaging content regularly. Optimizing your videos for SEO is critical by incorporating relevant keywords and tags.

2. Monetizing your channel:
Once you’ve amassed a sizable audience, you can begin monetizing your channel through various methods such as sponsored content, affiliate marketing, and YouTube’s Partner Program. When brands pay you to feature their products or services in your videos, this is known as sponsored content. Affiliate marketing is the practice of promoting someone else’s products or services in exchange for a commission on each sale made via your unique link. YouTube’s Partner Program allows you to earn money through advertisements, sponsorships, and other forms of monetization.

3. Making a product:
Making your product is another way to make money with YouTube Shorts. You can use your audience to promote and sell your product. This can range from an e-book to a course to a physical item.

4. Leveraging your audience:
Once you have a large audience, you can use it in other ways to make money. You can, for example, use your audience to promote your services like coaching or consulting. Through affiliate marketing, you can also use your audience to promote the products or services of others.

5. Diversifying your income streams:

Diversifying your income streams is critical for a long-term, sustainable business. In addition to monetizing your YouTube channel, you can expand your audience and earn money by using other platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, or Twitch. To further monetize your audience, you can sell merchandise, offer paid memberships, or create a subscription service.



YouTube Shorts is a great way to make money, but building a large audience takes time and effort. Once you’ve amassed a sizable following, you can monetize your channel through a variety of means, including sponsored content, affiliate marketing, and YouTube’s Partner Program. You can also create your product and use your audience to make money in other ways.
